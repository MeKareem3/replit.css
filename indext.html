Replit.css

Make your sites look like Replit, sort of.

A select few drop-in styles to make small websites look like Replit. It comes with:

  • Replit's navbar
  • Replit's buttons
  • Replit's color schemes (css variables)
  • Styled text/number/password inputs
  • Cards
  • Replit's border (.outline class)
  • A <replit> element for the Replit logo (color optional)
  • A LINE (see below)

How do I use it?

Simply fork this Repl or add the following stylesheet to your Repl! No crediting required.
